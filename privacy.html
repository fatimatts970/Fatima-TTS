Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

At Fatima TTS Studio, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us while using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or personal registration to use our text-to-speech service. The text you submit for conversion is processed solely to generate the requested audio output and is not used for any other purpose. We may collect limited technical information automatically, such as browser type, device information, and usage analytics, to help us improve platform performance and reliability.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used strictly to operate, maintain, and improve Fatima TTS Studio. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Generated Audio Files

Audio files generated through our platform are temporarily stored on our servers to allow playback and download, and are periodically removed as part of routine maintenance.

Cookies

Our website may use local browser storage to remember your favorite voice selections. This information is stored only on your own device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or content delivery. These providers may have access to limited technical data solely to perform services on our behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our Contact page.
